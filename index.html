The World of Bread

Bread has been a staple food for thousands of years, dating back to ancient civilizations like Egypt and Mesopotamia. It evolved from simple flatbreads to the diverse varieties we see today, influenced by different cultures and baking techniques.

Sourdough

Origin: Ancient Egypt

A naturally leavened bread with a tangy flavor.

Baguette

Origin: France

A long, thin loaf with a crispy crust and soft interior.

Rye Bread

Origin: Germany

Made with rye flour, often denser with a rich, earthy taste.

Whole Wheat

Origin: Worldwide

A healthier option made from whole wheat grains.

Brioche

Origin: France

A soft, buttery bread often used in desserts and pastries.
